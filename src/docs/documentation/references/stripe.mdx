# Setting up Stripe

Bubblin uses Stripe Connect for author payments. It is very easy to attach Stripe Connect with your Bubblin Account.

First, you must be logged in on Bubblin. Now, you must in editing mode, here simply hover on the `Pricing` tab on the top of the editor.
<div class="two-third center">
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/marvindanig/assets/master/stripe-connect-left.jpg" width="100%" />
</div>

Click the 'Set up Stripe Connect' button and follow through with the process at Stripe. You're all set.

### Settings page

You can also connect Stripe via the Settings page. Click on the `APIs` tab and then click `Connect Stripe` button to enable payments.
