# Signing Up
Signing up on Bubblin is a pretty easy task. Just follow these steps:

1. First, you are required to head to the [sign up page](https://bubblin.io/users/new).
2. Next, it requires you to fill in a few basic details which should be easy for you.
**(NOTE: Please keep your credentials safe, do not share your password with anyone.)**
3. Now click on the `Submit` button after filling all the necessary details.
4. Now, you will get an activation mail on your email account from Bubblin. Open that mail and click on `Confirm email address`.
5. That's it, you are now ready to read some awesome Superbooks at Bubblin.
