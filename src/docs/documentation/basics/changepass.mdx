# How to change your password?

Forgot your password? Suspecting someone might have stolen it? Don't worry, we've got you covered.

Just go to the [Settings Page](https://bubblin.io/settings#profile) of your profile.

<div class="two-third center">
  <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/marvindanig/assets/master/profile-menu.PNG" width="100%" />
</div>
Then go to the Security tab

<div class="two-third center">
  <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/marvindanig/assets/master/security-tab.PNG" width="100%" />
</div>

There, you will find the option to change your password.
