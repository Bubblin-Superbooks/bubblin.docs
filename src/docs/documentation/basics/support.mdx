# Support Information

Superbook on Bubblin must provide detailed info about supported
devices and the viewports they have been tested for.

> Providing this information on every Superbook is mandatory.

It helps the prospective reader understand quality of browser
support and potential issues for your book ahead and know exactly which
devices they can read your book on.
