# Front & Banner

### Front image:

Designing a cover for a Superbook is very easy.

Aspect ratio `450px X 610px` for front cover.

Use any of our own [front](https://github.com/marvindanig/official-handbook/blob/master/assets/images/cover.psd) PSD template to design one of your own. It's available under MIT License.
